###################################################
                  HOSTEL GUIDE 2025-2026
          Sunridge Institute of Technology (SIT)
###################################################

Welcome to the SIT Residential Campus.  
This guide provides information regarding hostel facilities, rules, fee structure, check-in/out procedures, and essential services.

---------------------------------------------------
1. HOSTEL TYPES & AVAILABILITY
---------------------------------------------------

Boys Hostels:
- Sunrise Block (First-Year Students) – 300 capacity
- Harmony Block (Senior Students) – 250 capacity

Girls Hostels:
- Orchid Block (First-Year Students) – 200 capacity
- Lotus Block (Senior Students) – 220 capacity

Room Types Available:
- Single Occupancy (Limited)
- Twin Sharing
- Triple Sharing

Allocation is done on:
- First come basis
- Academic year priority
- Scholarship/merit category (limited quota)


---------------------------------------------------
2. HOSTEL FEE DETAILS
---------------------------------------------------

Per Semester:
- Hostel Fee: INR 55,000
- Mess Charges: INR 28,000
- Laundry & Maintenance: INR 5,000
- Security Deposit (Refundable - One Time): INR 6,000

Late fee fine applies after due date: INR 100 per day.


---------------------------------------------------
3. CHECK-IN PROCEDURE
---------------------------------------------------

Students must carry:

✔ Admission Letter  
✔ Hostel Allotment Receipt  
✔ 2 Passport-size Photographs  
✔ Original ID Proof (Aadhar/Passport/Driving License)  
✔ Undertaking Form (signed by parent/guardian)  

Check-in timing:  
- 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM (Monday to Saturday)

After check-in, students will receive:
- Room key
- Mess card
- Hostel rule booklet


---------------------------------------------------
4. FACILITIES AVAILABLE
---------------------------------------------------

- Fully furnished rooms (Bed, Mattress, Table, Chair, Cupboard)
- 24/7 Wi-Fi Access
- Purified Drinking Water
- Study Room & Reading Hall
- TV & Common Lounge
- Gymnasium (Boys & Girls Separate)
- Indoor Sports Area (Chess, TT, Carrom)
- Outdoor Sports: Cricket Ground, Basketball & Volleyball Court
- 24/7 Security with CCTV Surveillance
- Resident Warden & Medical Nurse

Medical Assistance:
- On-campus clinic available (9 AM – 6 PM)
- Emergency ambulance available


---------------------------------------------------
5. MESS & DINING RULES
---------------------------------------------------

Mess Timings:
- Breakfast: 7:30 AM – 9:30 AM
- Lunch: 12:30 PM – 2:30 PM
- Dinner: 7:30 PM – 9:30 PM

Weekly menu changes every Sunday.  
Outside food delivery allowed only till 10 PM.

Food complaints or diet requests may be submitted via hostel portal.


---------------------------------------------------
6. HOSTEL RULES & CODE OF CONDUCT
---------------------------------------------------

✔ Attendance is mandatory for first-year students.  
✔ Entry Timing:  
   - First-year: 9:00 PM  
   - Senior students: 10:30 PM  

✔ Silence Hours: 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM  
✔ Visitors allowed only in designated areas (Only parents/guardians).  
✔ Alcohol, smoking, hookah, drugs, and gambling strictly prohibited.  
✔ Damage to property will be charged along with penalty.  
✔ Pets are not allowed.  
✔ Students must keep rooms clean and participate in weekly room inspections.  

Violation of rules may lead to disciplinary action, including hostel cancellation.


---------------------------------------------------
7. LEAVE & EXIT POLICY
---------------------------------------------------

Short Leave:
- Allowed with warden approval

Long Leave:
- Requires parent approval and academic permission

Hostel Exit:
- Security deposit refunded after room inspection
- All dues must be cleared before leaving
