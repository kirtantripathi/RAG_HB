The Sports Department at SIT promotes physical fitness, teamwork, competitive spirit, and student well-being.  
This document outlines available sports facilities, timings, membership rules, equipment borrowing policies, and conduct guidelines.

-----------------------------------------------------------
1. AVAILABLE SPORTS FACILITIES
-----------------------------------------------------------

Outdoor Facilities:
- Cricket Ground (Full Size)
- Football Ground
- Basketball Court (2 courts)
- Volleyball Court (2 courts)
- Tennis Court
- Synthetic Running Track (400m)
- Practice Nets for Cricket

Indoor Facilities:
- Table Tennis Hall (4 tables)
- Badminton Courts (3 courts)
- Chess & Carrom Room
- Yoga & Meditation Room
- Indoor Fitness Arena

Gym Facilities:
Separate gyms for — Boys & Girls  
- Cardio Section (Treadmills, Cycles)
- Strength Training Area
- CrossFit Zone
- Certified Gym Trainers

-----------------------------------------------------------
2. SPORTS COMPLEX TIMINGS
-----------------------------------------------------------

Weekdays (Mon – Fri):
- Morning Session: 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM
- Evening Session: 4:00 PM – 9:30 PM

Weekends (Sat – Sun):
- 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM (Open All Day)

Gym Timings:
- Boys: 6:00 AM – 8:00 AM & 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Girls: 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM & 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM

Timings may change during tournament seasons and exams.

-----------------------------------------------------------
3. EQUIPMENT BORROWING SYSTEM
-----------------------------------------------------------

✔ Valid Student ID Card required  
✔ One-time refundable security deposit: INR 500  
✔ Equipment borrowing limit: 2 items at a time  
✔ Borrowing Duration: Maximum 2 hours  

Late return penalty: INR 50 per hour.  
Lost or damaged items must be replaced or compensated.

-----------------------------------------------------------
4. SPORTS CLUBS & TEAMS
-----------------------------------------------------------

Students may join official SIT sports clubs based on interest:

- Cricket Club
- Football Club
- Athletics Club
- Basketball Club
- Volleyball Club
- Badminton Club
- Table Tennis Club
- Chess Club
- Yoga & Wellness Club

Selections for official college teams occur twice a year:
- August selections  
- January selections  

-----------------------------------------------------------
5. COMPETITIONS & EVENTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

SIT organizes:

✔ Annual Sports Week  
✔ Inter-Department Championships  
✔ Inter-College Sports Fest (SUNRIDGE CUP)  
✔ Fitness & Marathon Events  

Top performers may represent the institute at:
- District Level
- State Level
- National Inter-University Tournaments

-----------------------------------------------------------
6. RULES & CODE OF CONDUCT
-----------------------------------------------------------

✔ Proper sports attire and shoes are mandatory.  
✔ Respect coaches, referees, and fellow players.  
✔ Use of alcohol, drugs, smoking, or abusive language is strictly prohibited.  
✔ Maintain cleanliness and responsibly use equipment.  
✔ Medical clearance required for high-intensity participation if requested.

Violation of rules may result in suspension from facilities.

-----------------------------------------------------------
7. MEDICAL SUPPORT & SAFETY
-----------------------------------------------------------

- First-aid kits available at all major sports zones.  
- Physiotherapy assistance available twice a week.  
- Emergency ambulance service available on campus.  

Students with injuries must report to the sports coordinator.
