##################################################
                  LIBRARY RULES & GUIDELINES
        Sunridge Institute of Technology (SIT)
                     Academic Year 2025–2026
##################################################

Welcome to the SIT Central Library.  
The library is intended to support learning, research, academic development, and self-study.  
This document outlines membership procedures, borrowing rules, timings, and guidelines.

--------------------------------------------------
1. LIBRARY TIMINGS
--------------------------------------------------

Regular Days (Monday to Friday):
- 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Weekends (Saturday & Sunday):
- 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Exam Weeks:
- Open 24 Hours (Silent Study Hall Only)

Library remains closed on national holidays unless notified.


--------------------------------------------------
2. MEMBERSHIP & ACCESS
--------------------------------------------------

✔ All registered SIT students, faculty, and staff are eligible for membership.  
✔ Library ID card / Student ID is mandatory for entry.  
✔ ID card must be shown during borrowing and verification.  
✔ Membership is valid for the duration of the academic program.


--------------------------------------------------
3. BORROWING POLICY
--------------------------------------------------

Maximum Books Allowed:
- Undergraduate Students: 4 books
- Postgraduate Students: 6 books
- Research Scholars: 8 books
- Faculty Members: 10 books

Loan Duration:
- Students: 14 days
- Faculty: 30 days
- Reference books, journals, and newspapers: Not Issuable

Renewal:
- Books can be renewed once if not reserved by another user.
- Renewal must be done before due date via the library portal.

Lost or Damaged Books:
- Replacement of same edition or approved cost + fine is required.


--------------------------------------------------
4. FINES & PENALTIES
--------------------------------------------------

Late Return Fine:
- INR 5 per day per book (first 10 days)
- INR 15 per day after 10 days
- Suspension of library privileges if book overdue exceeds 30 days

Lost ID Fine:
- Duplicate ID Fee: INR 200 + verification

Misuse of library property may lead to disciplinary action.


--------------------------------------------------
5. DIGITAL RESOURCES & e-LIBRARY
--------------------------------------------------

The following digital facilities are available:

✔ eBooks & Research Databases  
✔ IEEE, Springer, Elsevier Access  
✔ Digital Thesis Repository  
✔ Online Reservation & Renewal System  

Access is available via campus Wi-Fi or student login credentials.


--------------------------------------------------
6. CONDUCT & SILENCE RULES
--------------------------------------------------

✔ Strict silence must be maintained inside reading halls.  
✔ Mobile phones must be on silent/vibration mode.  
✔ Loud conversations, group discussions, and video calls are strictly prohibited inside library.  
✔ Eating or drinking (except water) is not allowed.  
✔ Bags must be kept in the designated storage area.


--------------------------------------------------
7. COMPUTER & STUDY ROOM USAGE
--------------------------------------------------

Computer Lab Rules:
- Access allowed for academic use only.
- Time limit: 1 hour if others are waiting.
- External storage devices must be scanned before use.

Group Study Rooms:
- Available on reservation basis.
- Maximum duration: 2 hours per group.

--------------------------------------------------
8. EMERGENCY & SAFETY GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------------

- Follow exit signage in case of emergency.
- Tampering with fire safety equipment is strictly prohibited.
- Any suspicious activity must be reported to library staff immediately.
