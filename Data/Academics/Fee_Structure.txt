##################################################
              FEE STRUCTURE 2025–2026
      Sunridge Institute of Technology (SIT)
##################################################


--------------------------------------------
1. UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS (B.Tech - 4 Years)
--------------------------------------------

**Tuition Fee**
- Semester 1–8: INR 95,000 per semester

**Additional Fees**
- Examination Fee: INR 4,500 per semester
- Lab & Facility Charges: INR 6,000 per semester
- Library & Digital Access Fee: INR 2,500 per semester

**One-Time Fees (Payable during admission)**
- Admission Fee: INR 12,000 (Non-refundable)
- Security Deposit: INR 5,000 (Refundable)

**Estimated Yearly Cost**
- Tuition + Other Charges: ~ INR 2,22,000 per year


--------------------------------------------
2. POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMS (M.Tech - 2 Years)
--------------------------------------------

**Tuition Fee**
- Semester 1–4: INR 75,000 per semester

**Additional Fees**
- Examination Fee: INR 4,500 per semester
- Research & Lab Fee: INR 10,000 per semester

**One-Time Fees**
- Admission Fee: INR 10,000 (Non-refundable)
- Security Deposit: INR 5,000 (Refundable)

**Estimated Yearly Cost**
- Tuition + Other Charges: ~ INR 1,90,000 per year


--------------------------------------------
4. HOSTEL & MESS CHARGES (Optional)
--------------------------------------------

- Hostel Fee: INR 55,000 per semester
- Mess Charges: INR 28,000 per semester
- Laundry & Maintenance: INR 5,000 per semester
- One-Time Hostel Security: INR 6,000 (Refundable)

**Approx Annual Hostel Cost:** INR 1,78,000


--------------------------------------------
5. SCHOLARSHIP & FEE WAIVERS
--------------------------------------------

Merit-Based Scholarships:
- Above 95% in 12th Board / Rank < 5,000 (JEE/GATE):
  → 80% Tuition Fee Waiver
- 90–95% or Rank 5,000–12,000:
  → 50% Tuition Fee Waiver
- 80–90% or Rank 12,000–20,000:
  → 25% Tuition Fee Waiver

Need-Based Support:
- Available for eligible students after verification.

Government & External Scholarships:
- SC/ST/PWD scholarships applicable as per government norms.


--------------------------------------------
6. PAYMENT SCHEDULE
--------------------------------------------

Payments must be completed in the following timeline:

- Semester Fee Payment Deadline: 10 days before semester start
- Late Fee Penalty: INR 100 per day (up to 30 days max)
- Failure to pay within deadline may result in temporary suspension of classes, exam access, or hostel privileges.
