          ADMISSION GUIDE 2025-2026
      Sunridge Institute of Technology (SIT)
###############################################

Welcome to Sunridge Institute of Technology (SIT).  
This guide provides complete information about programs offered, eligibility criteria, admission process, required documents, important dates, and fee structure.

------------------------------------------------
1. PROGRAMS OFFERED
------------------------------------------------

Undergraduate Programs (4-Year):
- B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) - 180 seats

Postgraduate Programs (2-Year):
- M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence


------------------------------------------------
2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
------------------------------------------------

For B.Tech Programs:
- Must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- Minimum aggregate: 60% (General), 50% (SC/ST/PWD).
- Valid JEE Main score or SIT Entrance Exam score.

For M.Tech Programs:
- Must have completed B.Tech/BE in relevant discipline.
- Minimum aggregate: 55% (General), 50% (SC/ST/PWD).
- GATE score accepted. SIT PG Entrance Test required otherwise.


------------------------------------------------
3. ADMISSION PROCESS
------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Online Application
- Fill out the application form on the official website.
- Upload passport-sized photo and digital signature.
- Pay application fee INR 500 (Non-refundable).

Step 2: Entrance Examination
- Candidates must appear for either JEE Main / GATE / SIT Entrance Exam depending on the program.

Step 3: Counseling & Allotment
- Seats will be allotted based on merit, entrance score, and category.
- Candidates must confirm admission by paying the seat confirmation fee.

Step 4: Document Verification & Final Admission
- Physical verification of documents at the campus.
- Final admission confirmed after fee payment.

------------------------------------------------
4. DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
------------------------------------------------

- Class 10 and 12 Marksheet (Original + Photocopy)
- Transfer Certificate (TC)
- Entrance Exam Scorecard
- Passport-size photographs (6)
- Aadhar Card / Government ID Proof
- Category Certificate (if applicable)
- Migration Certificate (for out-of-state students)

------------------------------------------------
5. FEE STRUCTURE (2025-2026)
------------------------------------------------

B.Tech Programs:
- Tuition Fee: INR 95,000 per semester
- Hostel Fee (Optional): INR 55,000 per semester
- One-time admission charges: INR 12,000

M.Tech Programs:
- Tuition Fee: INR 75,000 per semester

Note: Scholarships available based on merit and category.
