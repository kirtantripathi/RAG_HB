College Attendance Policy  
Effective Academic Year: 2025–2026  

--------------------------------------
1. Minimum Attendance Requirement
--------------------------------------
- Students must maintain **at least 75% attendance** in every course (theory and lab separately).
- Attendance includes:
  ✔ Classroom lectures  
  ✔ Tutorials  
  ✔ Practical sessions  
  ✔ Department seminars  

Failure to meet the minimum requirement will result in:
- **Detention in the semester examination**, or  
- Mandatory **remedial assignments** and approval from department.

--------------------------------------
2. Leave and Absence Rules
--------------------------------------
- All leave requests must be submitted through the **Student Attendance Portal** or via email to the department coordinator.
- Medical leave is accepted only with:
  ✔ Valid medical certificate  
  ✔ Prescription from a registered doctor  

- Absence without approval will be marked **Absent-Unexcused (AU)**.

--------------------------------------
3. Special Considerations
--------------------------------------
Students participating in:
- National/State level sports
- Hackathons and recognized technical events
- NSS / NCC activities
- University representation

may receive attendance relaxation up to **10%**, subject to approval from:
- Faculty Advisor  
- Head of Department  
- Dean of Academics  

--------------------------------------
4. Late Entry and Early Exit
--------------------------------------
- Entry to class is allowed only during the first **5 minutes**.
- Late entry will be allowed only once per course per month.
- Leaving class without permission will be recorded as **Absent** for that session.

--------------------------------------
5. Attendance Display and Appeals
--------------------------------------
- Attendance records will be updated weekly and accessible through the student portal.
- Any discrepancies must be reported **within 7 days** to the course instructor.
- Students may formally appeal detention cases through the **Academic Review Committee**.

--------------------------------------
6. Consequences of Short Attendance
--------------------------------------
Attendance below:

- **75% but ≥ 60%** → Eligible for remedial coursework + penalty assignment.  
- **Below 60%** → Not eligible for semester exams; must repeat the course in the next term.

--------------------------------------
7. Attendance in Exams
--------------------------------------
- No student with **shortage of attendance** will be allowed to appear for:
  ✔ Internal assessments  
  ✔ Mid-semester exams  
  ✔ Final semester exams  

--------------------------------------
8. Responsibility
--------------------------------------
- It is the student’s responsibility to monitor and maintain attendance.
- Faculty and administration are not responsible for reminder notices.

--------------------------------------
Note:
The college reserves the right to revise this policy based on academic council decisions and regulatory guidelines.
