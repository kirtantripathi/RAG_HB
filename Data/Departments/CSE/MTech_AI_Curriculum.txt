###########################################################
                    M.TECH COMPUTER SCIENCE
                Curriculum Structure (2025–2027)
            Sunridge Institute of Technology (SIT)
###########################################################

Program Duration: 2 Years  
Total Semesters: 4  
Degree Title: Master of Technology (Computer Science & Engineering)

-----------------------------------------------------------
YEAR 1 — ADVANCED FOUNDATIONS & RESEARCH BASICS
-----------------------------------------------------------

SEMESTER 1
-----------
Core Subjects:
1. Advanced Algorithms & Optimization Techniques  
2. Advanced Operating Systems  
3. Statistical Methods for AI & Data Science  
4. Research Methodology & Technical Writing  

Electives (Choose One):
- Modern Computer Architecture
- Distributed Ledger & Blockchain Systems
- Wireless & Mobile Computing

Labs:
- Algorithm Simulation & Analysis Lab  
- Research Writing & Technical Tools Lab (LaTeX, Zotero, IEEE Format)

Credits: 22


SEMESTER 2
-----------
Core Subjects:
1. Machine Learning & Deep Neural Networks  
2. Advanced Database Systems & Data Engineering  
3. Cloud Computing & Virtualization Technologies  
4. High Performance Computing (HPC)  

Electives (Choose One):
- Natural Language Processing
- Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) Foundations
- Advanced Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics

Labs:
- Machine Learning & Neural Programming Lab  
- Cloud Deployment & Containerization Lab  

Industry Component:
- Mini Project / Industrial Internship (6–8 Weeks)

Credits: 23


-----------------------------------------------------------
YEAR 2 — SPECIALIZATION, RESEARCH, AND DISSERTATION
-----------------------------------------------------------

SEMESTER 3
-----------
Core Subjects:
1. Deep Learning Applications & Optimization  
2. Data Science for Decision Systems  
3. Research Seminar / Colloquium  

Electives (Choose One):
- Computer Vision & Image Processing
- Intelligent Robotics
- Edge & IoT Intelligence System Design
- Quantum Machine Learning  

Project Work:
- Thesis / Dissertation Phase–I (Proposal + Literature Review)

Credits: 20


SEMESTER 4
-----------
Core Subjects:
1. AI Ethics, Policy & Responsible Computing  
2. Entrepreneurship & Innovation in Emerging Technologies  

Electives (Choose One):
- Generative AI & Language Models  
- Autonomous Systems & Reinforcement Learning  
- Systems Security Engineering  

Project Work:
- Thesis / Dissertation Phase–II  
- Industry Thesis / Publication Target  

Credits: 18


-----------------------------------------------------------
GRADING & ASSESSMENT WEIGHTAGE
-----------------------------------------------------------

- Mid-Semester Exams: 20%  
- End Semester Exams: 40%  
- Continuous Internal Evaluation / Seminars: 20%  
- Research Work / Project Components: 20%  

Minimum passing requirement: 50% per subject and overall CGPA ≥ 6.0
